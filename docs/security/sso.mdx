## Supported SSO Providers

You can enable various SSO providers, including Google and GitHub, to integrate with your system. By configuring SSO.

## Enforcing SSO

Uou can enforce SSO by specifying the domain, As part of SSO configuration, you have the option to disable email and user login. This ensures that all authentication is routed through the designated SSO provider.
